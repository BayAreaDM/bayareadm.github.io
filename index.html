Bay Area Dance Marathon

Bay Area Dance Marathon is an exciting new initiative that invites young philanthropists to dance for a cause. Dancers commit to staying on their feet for eight life-changing hours to raise funds and awareness for UCSF Benioff Children’s Hospital Oakland, our local Children’s Miracle Network Hospital and safety net for all Bay Area children. Dance Marathon is a nationwide movement that empowers and encourages individuals to stand for those who can’t.
