BAY AREA DANCE MARATHON

Young professionals on a mission

We are Bay Area Dance Marathon.
We’re a team of young professionals here to change the lives of local children. We host events throughout the year to raise funds and awareness for UCSF Benioff Children’s Hospital Oakland, our Children’s Miracle Network Hospital. 100% of the funds raised by our group benefit the hospital. We think that’s pretty cool.

We're throwing a gala on October 24 at the Children's Creativity Museum. The event is from 7 p.m. to midnight and includes an open bar. Dress to impress. All proceeds benefit UCSF Benioff Children's Hospital Oakland. You in?

The Kids

100% of the funds raised by Bay Area Dance Marathon benefit Children’s Hospital Oakland. Those funds ensure the hospital provides the finest level of pediatric care to all children in our region - regardless of a family’s ability to pay.

The Hospital

Children’s Hospital Oakland serves over 200,000 kids each year. 50,000 come through the hospital’s emergency room, the only Level 1 Pediatric Trauma Center in the Bay Area. The hospital provides over $9 million in charity care every year.
