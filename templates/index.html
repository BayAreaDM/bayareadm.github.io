Children's Miracle NetworkCMNH

Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals began in 1983 in Salt Lake City, UT as a small television fundraiser in Provo, Utah, and has grown to become the world’s leading children’s charity, raising more than $2.2 billion for the 170 pediatric hospitals in its international alliance. Most of the funds have been donated $1 at a time through the sale of paper Miracle Balloons at corporate sponsors like Costco, Wal-Mart and Rite Aid, which have contributed more than $300 million to the Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals’ national program.
For more information about CMNH visit cmn.org.

UCSF Benioff Children'sUBCHO

For over 100 years, UCSF Benioff Children's Hospital Oakland has been delivering exceptional medical care to children from all regions of California. Caring for children is our number one priority, from routine check-ups and immunizations to the treatment of life-threatening injuries and illness. At Benioff Children's Hospital Oakland we stand by our mission; to protect and advance the health and well-being of children through clinical care, teaching and research.
For more information about UCSF Benioff Children’s Hospital Oakland visit childrenshospitaloakland.org.
